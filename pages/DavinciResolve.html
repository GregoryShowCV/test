Demo Reel.

Processes.

For the creation of the Demo Reel, it was decided to produce cinematics using Unreal Engine 5 through two Sequences.

  • The first Sequence features a guided camera movement from the kitchen to the basement’s iron-barred door.
  • The second Sequence focuses on the enemy performing the final attack animation, while the camera executes the shot along with the animation using keyframes and camera movements.

Additionally, aperture and focal length adjustments were used to enhance depth and create a more immersive composition.

Once the cinematics were obtained, they were imported into DaVinci Resolve.

  • Adjustment Clips were added to the video timeline to apply color corrections using nodes.
  • In the Color Grading section, Splitter & Combiner Nodes were used to manipulate the red and green channels, creating a VHS effect for the Demo Reel.
