Concept Art - Warrior.

① The warrior’s design combines elements of Japanese Yoroi armor and Chinese lamellar armor, but with creative liberties to emphasize the character’s uniqueness.

② The warrior’s weapon is also a hybrid design, blending a spear with a giant axe for a striking and powerful silhouette.

③ The environment represents a village in conflict, with the warrior serving as its protector.

① This is the rear pose of the warrior, showcasing the back view of their armor.

② The architecture of the columns is inspired by Torii gates, reinforcing the culture of the place

③ The setting aims to depict the entrance of a monastery.

Concept Art - Ancient Maiden.

① The kimono of the Maiden reflects her connection to the coastal environment, adorned with ocean creatures and waves

② The instrument she carries is a Shamisen, emphasizing her role as a musician and performer.

③ The setting portrays a serene coastline, reinforcing her origins.

① This pose is designed to express her carefree and free-spirited nature, showcasing fluidity in her movement.

② The scene places the Maiden on a sand dune, just before reaching the shore, enhancing the visual storytelling of her connection to the sea.
